Harry Potter Spells


From the time J.K. Rowling published her first book in the Harry Potter series—1998's Harry Potter and the Chamber of secrets—she introduced fans to an expansive new Wizarding World that fans have been lost in ever since. Potterheads have an eight movie film franchise that never gets old, inspiring quotes from the novels, an actual Universal theme park, and thousands of addictive fan fiction stories. And yet another element to add to the dynamic magical universe is a literal language created by Rowling, comprised of dozens of spells, charms, and hexes that Harry Potter wouldn't be complete without.

Muggles, witches, and wizards: Grab your wands, and enjoy!





  1. A


    1. Aberto - Opens locked doors
    2. Accio - Summons objects
    3. Aguamenti - Summons water
    4. Alohomora - Unlocks objects
    5. Anapneo - Clears someone's airway
    6. Aparecium - Reveals secret, written messages
    7. Apparate - A non-verbal transportation spell that allows a witch or wizard to instantly travel on the spot and appear at another location (disapparate is the opposite)
    8. Ascendio - Propells someone into the air
    9. Avis - Conjures a small flock of birds


    10. B


    11. Bat-Bogey Hex - Turns the target's boogers into bats
    12. Bombarda - Creates an explosion
    13. Brackium Emendo - Heals broken bones


    14. C


    15. Capacious Extremis - Known as the Extension Charm, it's a complicated spell that can greatly expand or extend the capacity of an object or space without affecting it externally
    16. Confundo - Known as the Confundus Charm, it causes confusion of the target
    17. Conjunctivitis Curse - Affects the eyes and sight of a target
    18. Crinus Muto - Changes hair color and style


    19. D


    20. Diffindo - Used to precisely cut an object
    21. Disillusionment Charm - Causes the target to take on the appearance of its surroundings
    22. Disapparate - A non-verbal transportation spell that allows a witch or wizard to instantly travel on the spot and leave for another location (apparate is the opposite)


    23. E


    24. Engorgio - Causes rapid growth in the targeted object
    25. Episkey - Heals minor injuries
    26. Expecto patronum - The Patronus Charm is a powerful projection of hope and happiness that drives away Dementors; a corpeal Patronus takes the the respective animal form of the caster, while a non-corpeal appears as a wisp of light; at 13, Harry Potter was the youngest known witch or wizard to prouduce a corpeal Patronus
    27. Erecto - Allows a witch or wizard to build a structure, like a tent
    28. Evanesco - Vanishes objects
    29. Expelliarmus - Forces an opponent to drop whatever's in their possession


    30. F


    31. Ferula - A healing charm that conjures wraps and bandages for wounds
    32. Fidelius Charm - A complex charm that conceals a secret into the soul of a chosen "Secret Keeper;" if a location is the subject of concealment, it becomes undetectable to others
    33. Fiendfyre Curse - Conjures destructive, enormous enchanted flames
    34. Finite Incantatem - A general counter-spell that's used to reverse or counter already cast charms
    35. Furnunculus Curse - A jinx that causes a breakout of boils or pimples


    36. G


    37. Geminio - Duplicates objects
    38. Glisseo - Transforms a staircase into a slide


    39. H


    40. Homenum Revelio - Reveals the presence of another person
    41. Homonculus Charm - Detects anyone's true identity and location on a piece of parchment; used to create the Marauder's Map


    42. I
    43. Immobulus - Immobilises living targets
    44. Impedimenta - A temporary jinx that slows the movement of the target
    45. Incarcerous - Conjures ropes
    46. Impervius - Makes an object waterproof
    47. Incendio - Conjures flames


    48. J
    49. Langlock - Causes the target's tongue to stick to the roof of their mouth
    50. Legilimens - Invading or navigating another's mind
    51. Levicorpus - Levitates the target by their ankle
    52. Locomotor Mortis - The Leg-Locker curse bounds the target's legs
    53. Lumos - Illuminates the caster's wand


    54. M
    55. *Morsmordre - Conjures and projects Lord Voldemort's Dark Mark
    56. Mucus Ad Nauseam - Inflicts an extreme runny nose or cold
    57. Muffliato - Creates a buzzing sound in the target's ears to prevent eavesdropping


    58. N
    59. Nox - Reverses the lumos charm, extinguishing a wand's light


    60. O
    62. Obliviate - Erases the target's memory
    63. Obscuro - Conjures a blindfold
    64. Oculus Reparo - Repairs eyeglasses
    65. Oppugno - Directs an object or person to attack a victim


    66. P
    67. Petrificus Totalus - Temporarily freezes or petrifies the body of the target
    68. Periculum - Conjures flares/red sparks
    69. Piertotum Locomotor - Incantation used to bring to life inanimate objects and artifacts
    70. Protean Charm - Links objects together for better communication
    71. Protego - Casts an invisible shield around the caster, protecting against spells and objects (except for The Killing Curse)


    72. R
    73. Reducto - Reduces the target to pieces
    74. Reducio - Shrinks an enlarged object to its regular size
    75. Renneverate - Awakens or revives the target
    76. Reparifors - Heals magical ailments like poisoning or paralysis
    77. Reparo - Fixes broken objects
    78. Rictusempra - A charm that disarms an opponent by tickling them
    79. Riddikulus - Used to defeat a Boggart, the charm allows the scary creature to assume a comedic form, disarming it


    80. S
    81. Scourgify - Cleans objects
    82. *Sectumsempra - Inflicts severe lacerations and haemorrhaging on the target
    83. Serpensortia - Conjures a live snake
    84. Silencio - Silences the target
    85. Sonorus - Amplifies the witch or wizard's voice
    86. Spongify - Softens the target
    87. Stupefy - The Stunning spell freezes objects and renders living targets unconscious


    88. T
    89. Tarantallegra - Aimed at the legs, causes uncontrollable dancing movement


    90. U
    92. Unbreakable Vow - A magically binding contract that results in the death of whoever breaks it


    93. W
    95. Wingardium Leviosa - Causes an object to levitate; but remember what Hermione said: "It’s Wing-gar-dium Levi-o-sa, make the 'gar' nice and long.’"
