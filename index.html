The Golden Trio

The Golden Trio, sometimes known as the Trio, is a popular name for the three main characters of the Harry Potter series. The Trio, hence the name, holds three members, being Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The trio is often allied with the Order of the Phoenix and Albus Dumbledore.
